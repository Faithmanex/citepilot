## 1. Service Description

CitePilot is an AI-powered academic citation consistency checking platform. The Service analyses uploaded documents to:

- Extract in-text citations and reference list entries
- Cross-reference citations against reference lists for consistency
- Validate references against external scholarly databases (Crossref, OpenAlex, PubMed, DOI.org)
- Detect potentially retracted sources via Retraction Watch integration
- Identify potentially fabricated or hallucinated citations
- Provide AI-generated explanations and suggested corrections
- Support multiple citation styles including APA 7, APA 6, Harvard, Vancouver, Chicago, MLA, IEEE, OSCOLA, and Turabian

The Service is available through web-based access at citepilot.com and, for eligible subscription tiers, through a RESTful API.
